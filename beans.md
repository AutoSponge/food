Common Instant Pot Bean Cooking Times (Unsoaked, High Pressure):
- Black Beans: 20–30 minutes
- Pinto Beans: 25–40 minutes
- Chickpeas/Garbanzo: 35–40 minutes
- Kidney Beans: 35–40 minutes
- Great Northern/White Beans: 25–35 minutes
- Lentils: 8–10 minutes 

Key Tips for Success:
- Natural Release is Key: Always allow the pressure to release naturally for at least 15–20 minutes to prevent the beans from exploding and to stop foam from clogging the valve.
- Soaking: Soaking reduces cooking time by about 10 minutes.
- Salt: Salt your water; it helps with flavor and does not make the skins tough, despite old myths.
- Ratio: Use at least 3-4 cups of liquid (water/broth) per 1 cup of dry beans.
- Capacity: Never fill the pot more than halfway to allow space for expansion.
