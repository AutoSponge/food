# Frittata (formula)

[Source](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-q8czZSQbbQ&t=291s)

**Steps**

1. heat non-stick skillet on medium with a little olive oil
1. Slice up and add 1-2 shallots
1. Add some thin cooked meat (e.g., bacon, prosciutto, ham, salami)
1. Add some chopped veggies (e.g., steamed broccoli)
1. 2-3 eggs per person w/ glug of milk or cream for texture, some shredded/crumbled cheese, and pepper whisked together
1. Pour in eggs and turn on broiler
1. Add some fresh herbs (e.g., dill)
1. Top with cheese and place under broiler

It's done when the eggs are set (soft but bouncy). Let it cool. Jiggle pan to release.
