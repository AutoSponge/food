# Bacon Bites

[Source](https://youtu.be/qZoYHHVNxr4?si=4ocgDGfedssJgtqv)

- Club crackers
- Thinly sliced Bacon and cut into strips that fit on the cracker
- brown sugar
- barbecue rub/whatever mix you want
- red pepper flake

bake at 350 for 25-35 min until bacon is crispy
