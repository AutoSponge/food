# Turkish Bread Topping

[Source](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eIDgcvpAvf8&t=455s)

## Ingredients (enough for 2 large pittas):

- 1 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
- 1 Tsp Pul Biber (Aleppo Pepper)
- 1/2 Tsp Oregano
- 1/2 Tsp sumac
- 1/4 Tsp paprika
- 1/4 Tsp salt
